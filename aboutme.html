My name is Ogwugbom Joseph Okpara Jr, but you can just call me Joseph. I was born in raised here in Oklahoma City. I grew up on the west side of OKC near the Bethany area. I attended high school at Putnam City West where I was a band nerd up until my senior year. I graduated in the summer of 2006 and started my undergrad in the fall at Oklahoma State University where I majored in Computer Engineering. After a short 5 year stint in college, I managed to graduate and get a job in the OKC Metro area working for the USAF at Tinker AFB. I currently work as an Electronics Engineer for a team that investigates software and hardware defficiencies in a production lab. I've been working there for a little over 7 years and obtained a master's degree from Oklahoma Christian University during my time. I've always had an interest in software and programming so now I'm taking action by doing OKCoders to learn more about software development to strengthen my skills and see where it takes me.
